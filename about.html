ABOUT US

OUR VISION:

To create a financially empowered uganda where every individual in pre-urban communities has easy, equitable access to savings and credit services.

MISSION:

To empower pre-urban and rural communities in uganda by providing accessible, member-owned savings and credit services that foster financial inclusion, economic resilience and sustainable livelihoods. As a cooperative we priotize equitable access and community-driven solutions to reduce poverty and among members.

GOALS:

By 2030, Achieve a 40% reduction in poverty among Alliance For Tomorrow members through sustained savings growth, responsible credit use and economic diversification.

OUR OBJECTIVES:

  • Boost Savings
  • Provide Loans
  • Train Members
  • Advocate and Partner
  • Credit access and Loan Distribution
